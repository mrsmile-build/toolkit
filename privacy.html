Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

At ToolKit, your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what information we collect and how we use it.

Information We Collect

ToolKit tools run entirely in your browser. We do not collect, store, or transmit any of the data you enter into our tools. Your text, numbers, and inputs never leave your device.

We may collect anonymous usage data through Google Analytics, including:

  • Pages you visit
  • Browser type and device type
  • General geographic region (country/city level)
  • Time spent on pages

Cookies

We use cookies through Google Analytics and Google AdSense to understand how visitors use our site and to display relevant advertisements. You can disable cookies in your browser settings.

Google AdSense

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google may use cookies and web beacons to serve ads based on your visits to this and other websites. You may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ad Settings.

Third-Party Links

Our site may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites.

Children's Privacy

ToolKit is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with a new date.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us via our contact page.
