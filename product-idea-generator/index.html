What is a Digital Product Idea Generator?

A digital product idea generator is an AI-powered tool that helps creators, entrepreneurs and side hustlers discover profitable ideas for ebooks, online courses, templates, newsletters and other digital products — based on any niche or topic they choose.

Instead of spending weeks researching what to create, you type your topic and get 8-10 ready-to-execute product ideas in seconds. Each idea comes with a suggested title, target audience, description and recommended selling price.

Why Digital Products Are the Best Way to Make Money Online

Digital products are files or content that people pay for and download — like ebooks, guides, templates, courses and checklists. They are the best side income for one simple reason: you create it once and sell it forever.

Unlike physical products, there is no shipping, no inventory and no manufacturing cost. Once your ebook or template is ready, every sale is almost pure profit.

People in Nigeria, Kenya, Ghana and across Africa are already making ₦100,000 to ₦500,000 monthly selling simple digital products on platforms like Selar and Gumroad — with nothing more than a phone and knowledge they already have.

Frequently Asked Questions

What types of digital products can I create?

You can create ebooks, online courses, templates, checklists, workbooks, newsletters, preset packs, swipe files, spreadsheets, Canva templates and more. The AI generator gives you specific ideas based on your niche with the best product type for each idea.

How much can I make selling digital products?

Income varies widely. Beginners typically earn ₦20,000 to ₦100,000 in their first month with consistent promotion. Experienced sellers earn ₦300,000 to over ₦1,000,000 monthly. The key is picking a high-demand niche, creating a quality product and promoting consistently on social media.

Do I need experience or skills to create digital products?

No. With AI tools like ChatGPT and Claude, anyone can create a professional ebook or guide in a few hours. You just need a topic, an AI writing tool and a platform like Selar or Gumroad to sell. This tool helps you find the right topic automatically.

Which platform is best for selling digital products in Nigeria?

Selar is the best platform for Nigerian sellers. It accepts Nigerian bank accounts, supports Paystack and Flutterwave, and has a growing community of African buyers. For global sales, Gumroad is the better choice as it accepts international cards and PayPal.

Is this digital product idea generator really free?

Yes. ToolKit is 100% free with no sign-up required. The AI generator, hashtag tool, platform strategy, monetization roadmap and 7-day content calendar are all free to use as many times as you want. No credit card, no account needed.
